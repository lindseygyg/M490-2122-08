Pick a level to start learning or practicing CPR!

Level 1 CPR

Novice: Understanding the fundamentals of basic CPR (includes surrounding check, pluse check and compression frequency).

Level 2 CPR

Beginner: Understand how to do chest compression properly (includes surrounding check, pluse check, compression frequency and compression depth).

Level 3 CPR

Intermediate: Understand when to prefrom rescue breaths (includes surrounding check, pluse check, compression frequency, compression depth, head tilt check, and rescue breath).

Level 4 CPR

Advanced: Understand where to place AED pad (includes surrounding check, pluse check, compression frequency, compression depth, head tilt check, rescue breath and AED placement).

Level 5 CPR

Expert: Includes Advanced components without guidance.

Level 5.5 CPR

Duration Expert: Includes Advanced components without guidance and repeats CPR steps multiple times

Level 6 CPR

Timed: Included Advanced components without guidance and adds a time element to provided a stress element to simulate a more relistic environment.

Individual Components

Option to train on different components of CPR manikin.
