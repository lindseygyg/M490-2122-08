Step 2: Begin Compressions

Assume there is no pulse. Since the person is not breathing and has no pulse, we will begin CPR. The first step is to start with chest compressions
You should do 30 compressions at a rate of 100 to 120 compressions per minute(or the song staying alive).
- it helps to count out loud.
Place your hands one on top of the other and interlace your fingers.
Place them on the sternum (middle of chest).
Ensure your arms remain straight and locked - begin compressions!
The arms should remain straight, so make sure the movement comes from the body.
